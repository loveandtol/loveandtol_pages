Contribute

There are no dues or fees, but we do have expenses. In keeping with the 7th tradition, you may contribute via Venmo, Cash.app, Zelle, or check.

What is the money used for?

The love and tolerance group follows the 7th tradition and uses any donations in the following manner:

  1. Expenses
    • Zoom fees
    • Website hosting fees
  2. Literature
    • We provide Big Books to anyone that requests them
    • Associated shipping fees
  3. Service org donations
    • We donate to both the General Service office and our local intergroup.
